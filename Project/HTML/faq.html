FAQs

What is Blast To The Past?

Blast To The Past provides a flawless, safe, and enjoyable time traveling experience.

Where in time can I go?

We take people wherever they want to go in time. Certain time periods are more dangerous and require more preparations, but we try our best to make sure our customers can go anywhere they want.

What precautions do you take in more dangerous time periods?

At Blast To The Past we have incredible cloaking technology that we provide to our customers to ensure that while in a different time period nobody can be seen or harmed.
