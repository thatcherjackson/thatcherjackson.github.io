FAQs

What is Blast To The Past

We are a company that strives to provide the best time-travel service in the world and give people the chance to experience places and times they otherwise would never get to.

Where can we go in time?

The only limit is your imagination. Over the years we have improved upon our services and processes to the point where we can take anyone anywhere in time! Exciting is isn't it?

What safety measures do you take?

For starters, we have a security team accompany every journey and maintain your safety from a distance. We also have someone equipped with an emergency return switch that brings everyone in the party back in an instant, if we feel a dangerous situation is arising. Not all places in time are pretty after all. We have never had any accidents, and we don't plan to. Your safety is our #1 priority.
