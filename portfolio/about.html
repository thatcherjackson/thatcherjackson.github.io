About

In my first semester of college at The College of Western Idaho, my goal was to just get the basic classes required for an associates, out of the way. In my second semester, I wanted to try to explore the other classes available to me and hopefully discover something that I was both passionate about and able to make a career out of. Luckily, I found that within the Software Development Program here at CWI and began working towards my Associate Degree in Software Development.

For almost 2 years now, I have been working on and refining my skills in many different programming languages such as Java, CSS, HTML, and C#, to the point where I have become comfortable with each.
